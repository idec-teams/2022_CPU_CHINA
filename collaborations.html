NIA Team Online Sharing Meeting

        CPU_CHINA actively exchanged projects with local Nanjing iGEM teams and participated in the NIA Team Online Sharing Meeting on 2022.06.04. There were six teams including NJU-China, NJMU-China and NJTech_China participated in this meeting. CPU_CHINA introduced its project and actively interacted with other teams in the question session. CPU_CHINA gained a lot from this meeting, the biggest gain is that through this sharing meeting we realized that the team has not estimated the cost of the final product, and after this meeting we will pay special attention to estimate the cost of the product.

China iGEM Online Meetup

        In order to improve the project and further improve it, CPU_CHINA participated in the China iGEM Online Meetup held by five schools including Guangxi University, Jiangnan University, Northwestern Polytechnical University and Jilin University from 2022.07.16 to 2022.07.17. There were 27 teams including ShanghaiTech-CHINA, NJU-CHINA and BUCT-CHINA presented their projects. In addition, the organizers invited iGEM official staff to answer questions for the participating teams. Through this Meetup, CPU_CHINA learned more about iGEM projects and planned to further communicate with interested iGEM teams.
